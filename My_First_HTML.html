How to Make a Sandwich

  1. Get Bread, Peanut Butter, Jelly, Knife and Plate
  2. Open bread and take out two pieces and place on plate
  3. Open Jelly and Peanut butter
  4. Use Knife to Spread Peanut Butter on one slice of bread
  5. Use knife to spread Jelly to other slice of bread
  6. Put Jelly side of one slice and peanut butter side of other slice together
  7. Enjoy Sandwich!
